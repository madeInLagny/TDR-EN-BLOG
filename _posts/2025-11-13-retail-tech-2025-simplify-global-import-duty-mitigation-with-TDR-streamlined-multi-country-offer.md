## What Does TDR Duty Drawback Solutions bring to Retail?
Duty drawback enables businesses to recover up to 100% of import duties, taxes, and fees paid on goods that are later exported, returned, or destroyed. For global retailers and e‑commerce merchants, these recoveries improve cash flow, reduce landed costs, and strengthen pricing competitiveness in international markets.
